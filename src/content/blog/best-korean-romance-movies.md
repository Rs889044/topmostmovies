Korean romance is its own emotional weather system. These films aren't shy about going for
the heart — and the best of them earn every tear with characters you genuinely believe in.
Whether you want a swoony date-night pick or a quiet cry, here's where to start.

## The Korean romance signature

Where Hollywood romance often races to the happy ending, Korean love stories like to *sit*
in feeling — longing, memory, the ache of timing. Time itself is a recurring co-star: films
fold in letters from the past, second chances, and love that has to outrun fate. The result
is romance with real stakes.

## Five to start with tonight

- **The Handmaiden (2016)** — sensual, twisty and beautifully made; the prestige pick.
- **Castaway on the Moon (2009)** — a strange, tender love story unlike any other.
- **Be With You (2018)** — the definitive modern tearjerker; bring tissues.
- **Decision to Leave (2022)** — Park Chan-wook's restrained, aching detective romance.
- **20th Century Girl (2022)** — first-love nostalgia done exactly right.
